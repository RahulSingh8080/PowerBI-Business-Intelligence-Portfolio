## Key Insights

- Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn rate  
- Competitor offers are the primary churn driver  
- Service dissatisfaction strongly increases churn probability  
- Customers with tenure < 12 months churn the most  
- Electronic check payment users show higher churn tendency  
- Geography has minimal impact on churn behavior
